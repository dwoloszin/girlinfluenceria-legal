Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026 · Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Girl Influenceria Publisher ("the App", "we") handles information. The App is a personal content-publishing tool operated by the Girl Influenceria channel to publish the channel's own AI-generated pictures and videos to the channel's own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram) through each platform's official API.

1. Information we collect

The App does not collect, store or process personal information from the public or from any third-party users. The only data the App handles is:

  • Authentication tokens (OAuth access/refresh tokens) that the platforms issue so the App can publish to the operator's own accounts. These tokens are stored locally on the operator's computer and are never transmitted to anyone other than the issuing platform.
  • Content and content metadata (AI-generated images and videos, captions, hashtags) created by the operator for the operator's own posts. The featured character is entirely virtual; no real person's likeness or personal data is used.

2. What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect data about visitors, viewers, followers or any other users.
  • We do not sell, rent, share or trade any data.
  • We do not use advertising, analytics or tracking technologies.
  • We do not process messages, contacts, friend lists or profile data of any user.

3. Data storage and security

Authentication tokens are stored locally on the operator's private computer and protected by the operating system's user account. They can be revoked at any time from each platform's security settings.

4. Data retention and deletion

Tokens are kept only while the App is in use. To delete all data, the operator removes the local token files and revokes the App's access in the connected platform's settings (e.g. TikTok → Settings → Security → Apps; Instagram/Meta → Apps and Websites). Since no third-party user data is collected, there is no third-party data to delete.

5. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone, including children.

6. Third-party platforms

Content is published to TikTok (ByteDance) and Instagram (Meta). Use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies. The App only interacts with them through their official APIs.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above will change and the current version will always be available at this URL.
